Escape Room

Solve puzzles and tasks to escape the room (Win).

Rules

Each team will consist of 2 Players
Both the players will be sent inside the room to solve puzzles at the same time
Once a team has completed the challenge its completion time will be noted and winners will be chosen upon fastest completion time.
Top two teams will be chosen as winners and runners up.
The organizers reserve the right to change the rules and regulations of the competition at any time.
All contestants are expected to comply with any changes made by the organizers. Prize money might change depending on entries . T&C applied

Additional Rules

If players are struggling with puzzles some hints will be provided but will cost addition to their timer.
Puzzles will be decided such that they could be solved in maximum of 10-15 mins.
A referee or volunteer will be present in the room to check the timing and provide hints as required.
Any form of cheating or malpractices will result in banning of the team.
Any futher instructions will be conveyed with time.
