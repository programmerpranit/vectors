Campus Calling

Clash Royale is a real time, head to head battle game set in the Clash Universe

Rules

Each team will consist of only 1 Player
Each player will have 3 minutes in which they have to earn crowns more than opponents
Players must first design an 8 - card tournament deck before taking part in the battle.
To make challenge more equitable for everyone, all cards levels, including the king Tower level, will be altered to same level.
From Quarter final round, each player will have right to change any 1 card from opponents deck.
Top two players will be chosen as winners and runner up.

Additional Rules

Only single entries are allowed.
If fake entries are noticed (double entry) it will lead to disqualification.
Any form of cheating or malpractices will result in banning of the team.
Any futher instructions will be conveyed with time.
