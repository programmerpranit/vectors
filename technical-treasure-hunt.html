Technical TREASURE HUNT

We are happy to announce that A.C Patil Clg of Engineering is bringing a unique treasure hunt experience for the young hearts of Acpcians, where a student will not only explore the beauty of college, but also will get to learn and realize various life values such as teamwork, time-management, presence of mind, logics & reasoning, empathy & a sense of helping others, etc…

Rules

1. The team has to solve the clues given to them and follow the tasks accordingly. To follow and complete the tasks, the team has to visit various landmarks . The presence of the team would be validly registered on each spot only when every participant would be present on the venue together.
2. The team or any of the team member would not interfere/indulge into the other team or member. The participant(s) doing so would be stated disqualified. In some cases, the organizing committee may decide to disqualify the entire team, which must be noted.
3. The team has to complete the quest in 3 hours. The top 2 teams which complete the quest the earliest and properly would be shortlisted for the winning teams

Disqualifications:

* Late Entry/Registration/Reporting;
*Having unfulfilled requirements related to identity or license;
*Reckless behaviour ;
*Damaging any property;
*Misbehaving; Interfering/indulging with any of the teams/team members;
*Stealing from other team/member, etc.
