TECHNICAL QUIZ

Rules

1. A team shall consist of max two students only.
2. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
3. Participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic devices.
4. Questions shall be in the form of multiple choice, True / False statement, Visuals, Specific- answer question etc.
5. Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.

Elimination Round

1. Each team would be given a set of question paper containing 20 multiple choice objective type questions.
2. Time limit - 20 minutes
3. Only 6 Teams would be selected for final stage rounds.
4. In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 5 questions would be asked for final selection.
5. The selected teams shall have to appear for the final round.

Final Quiz

★ Round 1: -
1. A question will be asked to a team and if they are unable to answer, it will be passed to the next team
2. Each team would be asked 2 questions each (Objective type or specific answer questions).
3. 10 marks for each correct answer.
4. If a team cannot answer the question, they can pass the question & then the question would be forwarded to the next team.
5. Answering time is only 20 seconds.
6. Team discussion is allowed.
★ Round 2 (Rapid Fire Round): -
1. Each team will be asked as many questions as possible one after another in one minute of time.
2. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer
3. Team discussion is allowed
4. If a team cannot answer the question, they can say pass for the next question. The question will not be forwarded to the next team.
★ Round 3 (Buzzer Round): -
1. 6 Questions (1 Round)
2. A question will be fired & the team who presses the buzzer first gets to answer first.
3. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong or if a team doesn’t answer after pressing the buzzer.
4. Unanswered questions will not be forwarded.
5. Answering time is only 05 seconds
★ Round 4 (Visual round): -
1. Teams will be shown clips and will have to answer.
2. 10 marks for each correct answer.
3. Each Team would be asked 2 visual questions. (1 slide in each round)
4. Visuals include hardware images, logos of Computer related product manufacturing, Business Companies etc.,
5. Answering time is only 20 seconds.
6. No passing to the next team. Audience can answer Finally.
