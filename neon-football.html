Neon Football

1-a-side futsal arena. Futsal lovers can now play the game in darkness, illuminated only by neon lights. With everything from the ball to the jerseys and goal posts in neon glow, it will surely be a unique experience for the players. To add more energy to it, loud music will be played while you play to keep you energetic and keep the game going.
