Circuit Nova

It's a mind-boggling competition where we want you to experiment and make something wonderful using your imagination and quiz-solving abilities. Participants in this competition will need to decode an electrical circuit part first and then create a functioning prototype. Three rounds make up this competition.
“Rapid Fire” this round will test student's knowledge about circuits and the basic components. “Circuit Sync” will help to evaluate student's Knowledge about the circuit and “Circuit Building” will evaluate student’s proficiency in circuit implementation.
A team of maximum 4 or at-least of 2 students can be formed. Event Consists of 3 Rounds.
With Two Eliminatory Rounds [Participants should crack down Round 1 to get into Round 2]
Round 2 winner will proceed into Final round of Circuit Nova.

Rules

Round 1: Rapid Fire.
10 Questions.
Each Question carries 5 points.
30-45 seconds will be provided for each Question.
Team who presses the buzzer first will get opportunity to answer the question. Else the question will be passed on to the next team.
Least scoring team will be eliminated at end of this round.
Round 2: Circuit sync
Identify Circuit from the clues provided within given time (2 min)*.
Least scoring team will be eliminated at end of the Round.
Round 3: Circuit Building
Implement given circuit on Bread board/PCB.
Winner will be decided as per the percentage of the total circuit they complete in give time.
